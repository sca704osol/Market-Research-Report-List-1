<p><p>The freezer bags market is categorized into three types based on polyethylene density: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE (High-density polyethylene) bags offer excellent strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy items. MDPE (Medium-density polyethylene) bags provide moderate flexibility and are suited for versatile applications. LDPE (Low-density polyethylene) bags are softer and more flexible, perfect for protecting delicate items. Each type caters to specific user needs, influencing their applications in food storage and transportation within the freezing environment.</p></p>
