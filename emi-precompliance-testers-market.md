<p><p>The EMI precompliance testers market encompasses several key segments. Radiated EMI testing assesses emissions from electronic devices through the air, while conducted EMI testing measures conducted noise along power or signal lines. Sensitivity testing determines a device's susceptibility to external electromagnetic interference, ensuring functionality under various conditions. Immunity testing evaluates how well a device can withstand electromagnetic disturbances, ensuring operational reliability. Together, these testing types help manufacturers ensure compliance with regulatory standards and enhance product performance in electromagnetic environments.</p></p>
