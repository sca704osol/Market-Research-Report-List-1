<p><p>Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNOs) are companies that offer mobile services without owning the network infrastructure. There are three primary market types: </p><p>1. Reseller MVNOs purchase bulk access from network providers and sell it under their brand.  </p><p>2. Service Operator MVNOs have more control, managing their customer service and billing while still relying on the host network for infrastructure.  </p><p>3. Full MVNOs possess their own core network elements, allowing them to offer customized services and greater operational independence.</p></p>
