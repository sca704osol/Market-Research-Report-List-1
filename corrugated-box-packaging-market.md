<p><p>The corrugated box packaging market is categorized into three main types: single, double, and triple corrugated boxes. Single corrugated boxes consist of a single layer of fluted material sandwiched between two liners, offering basic protection for lightweight items. Double corrugated boxes feature two layers of fluted material, providing enhanced durability and shock absorption, making them suitable for heavier products. Triple corrugated boxes, with three layers of fluted material, offer maximum strength and are ideal for very heavy or fragile items, ensuring secure transportation.</p></p>
