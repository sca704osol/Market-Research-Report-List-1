<p><p>Intranasal drug delivery devices are categorized primarily into multidose and uni/bidose systems. Multidose devices allow multiple administrations from a single container, providing convenience and cost-effectiveness for chronic conditions. These devices typically feature delivery systems that maintain drug stability over multiple uses. In contrast, uni/bidose devices are designed for single or double-dose administration, ensuring precise dosing and minimizing waste. Both types are essential for efficient drug delivery, enhancing patient adherence and therapeutic outcomes in various medical applications.</p></p>
