<p><p>Rigid-flex Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) combine rigid and flexible substrates, allowing for versatile designs in electronic applications. The market includes three main types: Single Sided Rigid-flex PCBs, which have one conductive layer and are ideal for compact devices; Double Sided Rigid-flex PCBs, featuring two conductive layers that enhance complexity and functionality; and Multi-layered Rigid-flex PCBs, which consist of several layers, offering advanced capabilities for high-density applications. These boards are essential for industries like aerospace, medical, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
