<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV speed sensor market is categorized into active and passive types. Active speed sensors generate their own signal using power, offering higher accuracy and reliability, often used in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Passive speed sensors rely on external magnetic fields to generate signals, are cost-effective, and commonly used in traditional systems. Both types are integral for monitoring wheel speed, ensuring safety, and optimizing performance in SUVs, with active sensors dominating modern, tech-driven vehicles and passive sensors in older or budget models.</p></p>
