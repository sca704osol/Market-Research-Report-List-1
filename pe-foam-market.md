<p><p>The PE foam market comprises three main types: IXPE, XPE, and EPE. IXPE (cross-linked polyethylene) is known for its superior durability, moisture resistance, and thermal insulation properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications. XPE (cross-linked polyethylene) offers good cushioning and thermal insulation, often used in packaging and construction. EPE (expanded polyethylene) is lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective, commonly utilized in packaging and protective applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing product performance and protection.</p></p>
