<p><p>Digital fabric printing machines comprise several types tailored for specific printing needs. Sublimation inkjet printing uses heat to transfer dye onto fabric, ideal for synthetic materials. Direct to fabric printing applies ink directly onto fabric, suitable for diverse materials and producing vibrant colors. Direct to garment printing, on the other hand, targets textiles such as cotton, allowing for detailed designs on finished garments. Each method caters to different applications, from fashion to interior decor, enhancing customization and production efficiency.</p></p>
