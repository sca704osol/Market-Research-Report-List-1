Cross-Section Milling involves the precise removal of material from samples to reveal internal structures, making it ideal for materials science and semiconductor applications. Flat Surface Milling, on the other hand, focuses on creating smooth, uniform surfaces on samples, enhancing surface characteristics for various analyses. Both techniques are essential in research and manufacturing, catering to industries such as electronics, materials engineering, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
