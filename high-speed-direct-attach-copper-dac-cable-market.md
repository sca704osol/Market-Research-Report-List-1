<p><p>High-Speed Direct Attach Copper (DAC) cables are used for short-range data connections in networking environments. Passive DAC cables rely on electrical signals without amplification, offering a cost-effective solution for short distances, typically up to 5-7 meters. In contrast, Active DAC cables incorporate built-in electronics to boost signals, enabling longer distances up to 15-20 meters while maintaining data integrity. Both types cater to high-speed applications, with Active cables providing better performance in more demanding settings.</p></p>
