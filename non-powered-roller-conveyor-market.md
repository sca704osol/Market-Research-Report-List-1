<p><ul><li>Chain-Driven Roller Conveyors</li><li>Belt-Driven Live Roller Conveyors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-powered roller conveyor market includes various types, notably chain-driven and belt-driven live roller conveyors. Chain-driven roller conveyors utilize chains to drive rollers, providing consistent movement for heavy loads over long distances. In contrast, belt-driven live roller conveyors use a belt that drives the rollers, enabling smoother handling of lighter loads and making them ideal for sorting and accumulation tasks. Both systems enhance efficiency in material handling, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
