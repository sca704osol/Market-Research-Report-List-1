<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bus Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus Starter Market includes Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control systems. Direct Control starters use manual switches to engage the starter motor, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but requiring physical effort. Electromagnetic Control starters use solenoids and relays for automated operation, providing convenience, precision, and reduced wear on components. Both types are essential for initiating bus engines, with Direct Control being common in older or smaller vehicles, while Electromagnetic Control dominates modern, larger buses due to its efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
