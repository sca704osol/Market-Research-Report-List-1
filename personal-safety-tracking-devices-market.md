<p><p>The personal safety tracking devices market primarily comprises two types: GPS and Bluetooth technology. GPS tracking devices utilize satellite signals to provide real-time location data over extensive distances, making them ideal for outdoor activities and emergency situations. In contrast, Bluetooth trackers offer proximity-based tracking, primarily connecting to smartphones within a limited range, suitable for everyday use like locating keys or personal belongings. Together, these technologies enhance personal safety through location awareness and quick retrieval capabilities.</p></p>
