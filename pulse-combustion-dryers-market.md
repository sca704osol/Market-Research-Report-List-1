<p><p>The pulse combustion dryers market comprises various types, including spray dryers, rotary dryers, and pneumatic dryers. Spray dryers utilize a fine spray of liquid feed, rapidly evaporating moisture in hot air, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Rotary dryers feature a rotating drum to gently process bulk materials, ensuring consistent drying. Pneumatic dryers rely on air flow to transport and dry particulate materials through pipelines, making them efficient for powdered products. Each type serves specific applications within industries requiring effective moisture removal.</p></p>
