<p><strong>The Microtunnel Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT)</li><li>Auger Boring</li><li>Vacuum Extraction TBM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microtunnel machine market includes various types designed for specific tunneling applications. Earth Pressure Balance Machines utilize controlled soil pressure to minimize ground movement. Slurry Shield (SS) machines use a slurry to support the tunnel face, particularly in soft soil conditions. Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT) involves first boring a pilot hole, then enlarging it with additional equipment. Auger Boring employs a rotating auger for material removal. Vacuum Extraction TBM uses suction to transport excavated material, while 'Others' encompasses various niche technologies and adaptations in the market.</p></p>
