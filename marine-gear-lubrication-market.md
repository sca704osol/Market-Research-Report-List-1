<p><p>The marine gear lubrication market primarily consists of two types: mineral lubricants and synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from refining crude oil, offering good performance and cost-effectiveness for various marine applications. They are widely used due to their availability and reliability. Conversely, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for enhanced performance under extreme conditions, providing superior protection against wear, corrosion, and thermal degradation. These lubricants cater to high-performance vessels, contributing to longer equipment life and improved fuel efficiency.</p></p>
