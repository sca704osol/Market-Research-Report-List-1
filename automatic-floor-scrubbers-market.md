<p><p>The automatic floor scrubber market consists of fully automatic and semi-automatic types. Fully automatic scrubbers operate independently, utilizing advanced sensors and navigation systems to clean floors without manual intervention. These machines are ideal for large spaces, enhancing efficiency and productivity. In contrast, semi-automatic scrubbers require some operator involvement, such as steering or handling controls, but still automate key cleaning functions. They are suited for smaller areas or tasks where flexibility and operator control are beneficial. Both types cater to diverse cleaning needs across various industries.</p></p>
