<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Time of Flight ToF Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF-Modulated Light Sources</li><li>Range Gated Imagers</li><li>Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are categorized into three main types: RF-modulated light sources, range-gated imagers, and direct time-of-flight imagers. RF-modulated light sources utilize modulated signals to calculate distances based on phase differences. Range-gated imagers capture images at set intervals, enabling depth perception through time-delay measurements. Direct time-of-flight imagers emit light pulses and measure the return time, providing highly accurate distance readings. Each type has distinct applications in robotics, automotive, and consumer electronics, enhancing spatial awareness and object detection.</p></p>
