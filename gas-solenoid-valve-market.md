<p><p>The gas solenoid valve market can be segmented into two main types: automatic and manual reset valves. Automatic solenoid valves operate without human intervention, using electrical signals to control gas flow, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and convenience. In contrast, manual reset solenoid valves require an operator to physically reset the valve after activation, offering a safety feature that prevents unintended gas flow. Both types serve critical roles in industrial, residential, and commercial gas management systems.</p></p>
