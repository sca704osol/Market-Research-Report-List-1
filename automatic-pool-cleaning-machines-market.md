<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning machines market is categorized into three main types: suction-side pool cleaners, pressure-side pool cleaners, and electric pool cleaners. Suction-side cleaners use the pool's existing suction to capture debris, making them cost-effective and easy to operate. Pressure-side cleaners utilize water pressure from the filtration system to power their movement and collect dirt, often incorporating a separate bag for debris. Electric pool cleaners are self-contained, powered by electricity, offering advanced features and comprehensive cleaning through programmable settings.</p></p>
