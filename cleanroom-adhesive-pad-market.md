<p><p>Cleanroom adhesive pads are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their material composition and applications in controlled environments. Type I pads are typically made from polyester or polyethylene, designed for low-contamination areas, ideal for cleanrooms in electronics and pharmaceuticals. Type II pads, often composed of more advanced materials like polyurethane, offer higher levels of particle retention and are suitable for more stringent cleanroom standards. Both types ensure effective contamination control while maintaining surface integrity.</p></p>
