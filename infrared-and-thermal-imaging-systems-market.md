<p><p>Infrared and thermal imaging systems are classified into two main market types: cooled and uncooled. Cooled thermal imaging systems utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and provide high-resolution images, making them ideal for demanding applications like military and aerospace. On the other hand, uncooled thermal imaging systems operate at ambient temperatures and are more compact, cost-effective, and energy-efficient, suitable for commercial and industrial uses. Both types serve diverse applications, including surveillance, maintenance, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
