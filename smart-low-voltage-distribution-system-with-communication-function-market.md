<p><p>Smart Low Voltage Distribution Systems (SLVDS) integrate communication capabilities to enhance the management of electricity distribution. The market types include Profibus, Profinet, and Modbus, which are communication protocols that enable real-time data exchange between devices. Profibus is widely used in process automation, offering robust performance. Profinet provides Ethernet-based solutions for industrial applications with higher data transfer rates. Modbus, known for its simplicity, is ideal for connecting various devices across different systems, facilitating interoperability and efficient monitoring.</p></p>
