<p><p>Net weight filling equipment is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual equipment relies on operators to fill containers, offering simplicity and low cost but requiring more labor. Semi-automatic systems combine manual input with automated functions, increasing efficiency while still involving some human oversight. Automatic filling equipment fully automates the process, ensuring high-speed, accurate fills with minimal labor intervention, making it ideal for large-scale production environments. Each type addresses different operational needs and production volumes.</p></p>
