<p><ul><li>Single Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Two Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid ring pump market includes two primary types: single-stage rotary vane vacuum pumps and two-stage rotary vane vacuum pumps. Single-stage rotary vane pumps operate with one rotor and are suitable for lower vacuum applications, offering efficiency and ease of maintenance. In contrast, two-stage rotary vane pumps employ two rotors to achieve deeper vacuum levels, making them ideal for more demanding applications. Both types are valued for their reliability and ability to handle vapor and liquid simultaneously, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
