<p><ul><li>RF Generators</li><li>Reusable Products</li><li>Disposable Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiofrequency ablation (RFA) devices for pain management are categorized into three main types: RF generators, reusable products, and disposable products. RF generators are essential for producing the radiofrequency energy needed for ablation procedures. Reusable products include electrodes and probes that can be sterilized and used multiple times, offering cost-effectiveness. Disposable products, on the other hand, are single-use items designed for convenience and to eliminate the risk of contamination, ensuring patient safety during procedures aimed at alleviating chronic pain.</p></p>
