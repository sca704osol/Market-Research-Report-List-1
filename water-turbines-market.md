<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Water Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Water Turbines</li><li>Reactive Water Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water turbines market is divided into two main types: impulse water turbines and reactive water turbines. Impulse turbines, such as Pelton wheels, convert the kinetic energy of water jets into mechanical energy, making them suitable for high-head applications. Reactive turbines, including Kaplan and Francis types, operate by utilizing both the pressure and kinetic energy of water, allowing for increased efficiency in medium to low-head situations. Each type serves distinct operational conditions, impacting their applications in hydroelectric power generation.</p></p>
