<p><p>The reusable blood dialyzer market comprises three primary types: Hollow Fiber, Flat Type, and Coil Type. Hollow Fiber dialyzers utilize thin, fiber-like membranes to effectively filter blood, offering high surface area and efficiency. Flat Type dialyzers feature flat membranes, providing straightforward blood flow paths and easier cleaning. Coil Type dialyzers, designed in a coiled configuration, optimize space and enhance flow dynamics. Each type serves varying patient needs and treatment settings, contributing to advancements in renal care.</p></p>
