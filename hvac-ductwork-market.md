<p><p>The HVAC ductwork market is segmented into three main types: sheet metal ducts, flexible non-metallic ducts, and fiberglass ducts. Sheet metal ducts, typically made of galvanized steel or aluminum, offer durability and efficiency in airflow. Flexible non-metallic ducts are lightweight and easy to install, making them ideal for tight spaces. Fiberglass ducts are designed for sound insulation and thermal efficiency, often used in energy-efficient systems. Each type caters to specific installation needs, performance criteria, and budget considerations.</p></p>
