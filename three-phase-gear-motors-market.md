<p><strong>The Three-phase Gear Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid-shaft Gear Motor</li><li>Hollow-shaft Gear Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase gear motors market primarily comprises solid-shaft and hollow-shaft gear motors. Solid-shaft gear motors feature a continuous shaft that transmits torque directly, making them ideal for rigid mounting in various applications. In contrast, hollow-shaft gear motors incorporate a hollow output shaft, allowing for easier integration with existing machinery and accommodating shaft extensions. Both types serve crucial roles in industrial automation, providing efficient power transmission and precise control in equipment such as conveyors, mixers, and pumps.</p></p>
