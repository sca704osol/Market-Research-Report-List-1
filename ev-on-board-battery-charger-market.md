<p><p>The EV On Board Battery Charger market can be divided into two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional chargers. Unidirectional chargers only allow electricity to flow from the grid to the vehicle's battery, enabling traditional charging. In contrast, bidirectional chargers allow energy to flow both ways, enabling the vehicle to not only be charged but also to discharge energy back into the grid or power home appliances. This flexibility supports grid management and energy efficiency, enhancing the overall sustainability of electric vehicle usage.</p></p>
