<p><p>The fiberglass yarn market primarily consists of two main types: fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn is produced by spinning strands of glass fibers, making it ideal for applications requiring flexibility and strength, such as textiles and composites. Fiber roving, on the other hand, is a collection of untwisted glass fibers, commonly used as a reinforcement material in various composite processes due to its superior strength and lightweight properties. Together, they cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing material performance and durability.</p></p>
