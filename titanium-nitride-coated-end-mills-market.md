<p><p>Titanium Nitride (TiN) coated end mills are categorized into three main types based on their cutting edges: peripheral cutting edge, end cutting edge, and others. Peripheral cutting edge end mills primarily engage in contouring and profiling, suitable for side milling. End cutting edge end mills are designed for vertical cutting and plunging into materials. Other types may include ball nose and tapered end mills, which serve specialized tasks. The TiN coating enhances durability, reduces friction, and improves surface finish in machining applications.</p></p>
