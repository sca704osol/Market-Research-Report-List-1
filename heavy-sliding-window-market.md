<p><p>Heavy sliding windows are categorized into single glazing and double glazing markets based on their insulation and energy efficiency features. Single glazing consists of a single pane of glass, providing basic insulation but less energy efficiency. In contrast, double glazing incorporates two panes with an air gap, significantly enhancing insulation and reducing heat loss. This makes double glazing more popular for energy-conscious consumers seeking better thermal performance, noise reduction, and overall comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
