<p><strong>The Polycarbonate Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Polycarbonate Membrane</li><li>Hydrophobic Polycarbonate Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate membrane market is divided into two main types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic membranes. Hydrophilic polycarbonate membranes are designed to attract water molecules, facilitating processes like filtration and separation, making them ideal for applications in biotechnology and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, hydrophobic polycarbonate membranes repel water, allowing gases and vapors to pass while blocking liquid, making them suitable for applications in microfiltration and air filtration systems. Both types serve critical roles in various industrial and research settings.</p></p>
