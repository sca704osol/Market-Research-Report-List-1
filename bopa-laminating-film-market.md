<p><p>BOPA laminating film is produced using two primary methods: flat-film extrusion and tube film extrusion. In flat-film extrusion, polymers are melted and formed into flat sheets, ideal for applications requiring specific thickness and surface properties. Conversely, tube film extrusion involves creating a tubular film that can be processed into various packaging solutions. Both methods cater to the growing demand for high-performance barrier films in packaging, offering excellent mechanical strength, clarity, and resistance to moisture, making them suitable for diverse applications.</p></p>
