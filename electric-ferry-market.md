<p><ul><li>Pure Electric</li><li>Hybrid Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Ferry market comprises two main types: Pure Electric and Hybrid Power. Pure Electric ferries rely entirely on battery-powered electric propulsion, offering zero emissions and quiet operation, ideal for short to medium routes. Hybrid Power ferries combine electric propulsion with internal combustion engines, providing flexibility for longer routes and reducing emissions compared to traditional ferries. Both types aim to enhance sustainability, with Pure Electric focusing on complete electrification and Hybrid Power balancing efficiency and environmental impact.</p></p>
