<p><p>Diamond slurries are abrasive suspensions used for polishing and precision machining. **Water-based** slurries are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and ideal for general applications. **Lubricant-based** slurries enhance surface finish and reduce friction, suitable for high-precision tasks. **Alcohol-based** slurries evaporate quickly, minimizing residue and are used in cleanroom environments. **Oil-based** slurries provide superior lubrication and stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications and materials requiring prolonged polishing. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
