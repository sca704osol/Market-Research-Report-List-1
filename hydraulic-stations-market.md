Swing hydraulic stations utilize pivoting mechanisms for efficient motion, while piston systems operate using cylinder-based force for lifting and pushing applications. Plunger hydraulic stations feature a direct push mechanism with a cylindrical plunger, ideal for precise control. Telescopic hydraulic systems consist of multiple extending sections, providing compact storage and extended reach, making them suitable for construction and heavy machinery operations. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
