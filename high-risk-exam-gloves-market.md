<p><p>The high-risk exam gloves market comprises two main types: high-risk latex exam gloves and high-risk nitrile exam gloves. High-risk latex exam gloves offer excellent elasticity and sensitivity, making them suitable for tasks requiring precision but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Conversely, high-risk nitrile exam gloves provide enhanced puncture resistance and a latex-free alternative, making them ideal for users with allergies and in scenarios where chemical exposure is a concern. Both types play crucial roles in protecting healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
