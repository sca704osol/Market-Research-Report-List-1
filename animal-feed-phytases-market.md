<p><p>Animal feed phytases are enzymes that aid in the hydrolysis of phytic acid, a form of phosphorus that is largely indigestible by monogastric animals. This not only contributes to better animal health and growth performance but also addresses environmental concerns associated with phosphorus runoff.</p><p>The Animal Feed Phytases Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period.
