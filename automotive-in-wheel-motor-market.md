<p><strong>The Automotive In-wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Rotor Type</li><li>Inner Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive in-wheel motor market comprises two primary types: outer rotor and inner rotor. The outer rotor type features the rotor on the outside of the stator, allowing for a larger torque generation and better heat dissipation, ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, the inner rotor type positions the rotor inside the stator, resulting in a compact design that enhances vehicle aerodynamics and reduces weight, making it suitable for passenger cars and efficiency-focused electric vehicles. Both types contribute to the evolution of electric drivetrain technologies.</p></p>
