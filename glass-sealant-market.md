<p><p>The glass sealant market is primarily divided into two types: single component and multi-component sealants. Single component sealants are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient and easy to apply. Multi-component sealants consist of two or more parts that must be mixed before application, offering enhanced performance and customization for specific applications. Both types cater to diverse needs in automotive, construction, and industrial sectors, providing essential sealing solutions for glass installations.</p></p>
