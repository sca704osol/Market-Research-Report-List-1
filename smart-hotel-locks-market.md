<p><p>The smart hotel locks market includes various types, primarily RFID card locks, magnetic card locks, and smart card locks. RFID card locks use radio-frequency identification technology for secure guest access, offering convenience and durability. Magnetic card locks rely on magnetic stripes, commonly seen in traditional hotel key systems, but are less secure than newer options. Smart card locks provide enhanced security by utilizing digital protocols and can integrate with mobile devices, allowing for innovative guest experiences and streamlined operations.</p></p>
