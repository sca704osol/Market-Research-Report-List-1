<p><strong>The Handheld X Ray Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DR</li><li>CR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld X-ray imaging device market is primarily segmented into two types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Radiography (CR). DR utilizes digital sensors to capture images directly, offering faster processing times and superior image quality, making it ideal for immediate diagnostics. In contrast, CR employs phosphor plates to capture X-ray images, which are then scanned and digitized, providing a more flexible and cost-effective solution, especially in settings with limited budgets or equipment. Both technologies cater to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
