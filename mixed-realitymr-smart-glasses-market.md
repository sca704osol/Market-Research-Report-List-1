<p><p>Mixed Reality (MR) smart glasses are categorized primarily into two types: All-in-one and Split type. All-in-one MR glasses integrate processing, sensors, and displays into a single device, offering a compact and user-friendly experience, ideal for personal and professional use. In contrast, Split type MR systems consist of separate components, such as a head-mounted display and an external processing unit, allowing for enhanced performance and customization, often catering to industrial applications or specialized tasks requiring more computational power.</p></p>
