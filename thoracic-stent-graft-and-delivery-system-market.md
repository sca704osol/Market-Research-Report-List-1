<p><p>The Thoracic Stent-Graft and Delivery System is a sophisticated medical device designed to treat aortic diseases, including thoracic aortic aneurysms and dissections. It consists of a tubular graft made of biocompatible materials that is supported by a stent, allowing for minimally invasive placement within the thoracic aorta. This system aids in reinforcement of the aorta, preventing rupture and facilitating proper blood flow.</p><p>The market for Thoracic Stent-Graft and Delivery Systems is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of aortic conditions, advancements in technology, and a rising geriatric population.
