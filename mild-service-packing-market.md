<p><ul><li>"Eptfe"</li><li>"Graphite Fiber"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mild Service Packing market includes two primary types: ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) and graphite fiber. ePTFE packing is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it suitable for various sealing applications in industries such as chemical processing and oil refining. Graphite fiber packing, on the other hand, offers high-temperature resistance and excellent strength, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications like high-pressure pumps and valves. Both types provide reliable sealing solutions tailored to specific operating conditions.</p></p>
