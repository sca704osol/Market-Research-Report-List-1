<p><p>Bagging machines for granulate materials are crucial for efficient packaging in various industries. The vertical form-fill-seal (V-FFS) machines create bags from a flat film, filling and sealing vertically, ideal for high-speed production and minimal floor space. In contrast, horizontal form-fill-seal (H-FFS) machines operate horizontally, providing versatility for bulkier granulates and complex packaging designs. Both systems enhance productivity and consistency, catering to diverse market needs in packaging granule products like fertilizers, food ingredients, and chemicals.</p></p>
