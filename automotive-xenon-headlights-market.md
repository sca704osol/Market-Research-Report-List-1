<p><p>The automotive xenon headlights market is divided into low beam and high beam lights, each serving distinct purposes for vehicle illumination. Low beam lights provide a directed, less intense light suitable for regular driving conditions, enhancing visibility without blinding oncoming traffic. In contrast, high beam lights offer a more intense and broader illumination range, designed for optimal visibility on dark, empty roads. Both types utilize xenon gas technology, which improves brightness and efficiency compared to traditional halogen bulbs.</p></p>
