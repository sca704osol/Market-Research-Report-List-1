<p><p>Native collagen refers to the natural, unaltered form of collagen, a structural protein found in connective tissues of animals and humans. Native collagen retains its triple-helix structure, making it highly effective for applications like wound healing, anti-aging products, and dietary supplements.</p><p>The Native Collagen Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for anti-aging and skincare products, rising awareness of health and wellness, and advancements in biotechnology. As consumer demand for natural and effective solutions continues to rise, the native collagen market is poised for robust expansion.</p></p>
