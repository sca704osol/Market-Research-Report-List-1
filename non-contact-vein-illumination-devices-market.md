<p><ul><li>Hyper-Spectral Sensor</li><li>RDAV</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact vein illumination devices utilize various technologies to visualize veins without direct contact. Hyper-spectral sensors analyze reflected light from tissues to identify blood vessels based on their unique spectral signatures, providing high-resolution images. RDAV (Reflective Digital Aerial Vein imaging) employs reflective methods to highlight vein patterns on the skin's surface, enhancing visibility. Other types may include infrared or near-infrared systems that leverage light to illuminate veins, offering safe and efficient options for medical professionals during procedures such as venipuncture.</p></p>
