<p><ul><li>Optical Fingerprint Lock</li><li>Semiconductor Fingerprint Lock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Visible Fingerprint Lock market is primarily divided into two types: optical fingerprint locks and semiconductor fingerprint locks. Optical fingerprint locks use light to capture an image of the fingerprint, offering high accuracy and robust security but requiring clear visibility of the scanner. In contrast, semiconductor fingerprint locks rely on capacitive sensors to detect the unique patterns of fingerprints, providing a compact design and better performance in various conditions, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
