<p><p>The Immuno-Oncology Treatment Market encompasses various types of therapies designed to enhance the body's immune response against cancer. Immune checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that stop the immune system from attacking cancer cells. Monoclonal antibodies are engineered to specifically target cancer cells. Cytokine-based immunotherapy uses immune signaling molecules to boost immune activity. Cancer vaccines aim to stimulate an immune response against tumor antigens. CAR-T cell therapy involves modifying a patient’s T cells to improve their ability to fight cancer.</p></p>
