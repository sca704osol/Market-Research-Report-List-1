<p><p>The One Time Password (OTP) display card market encompasses two main types: OTP magnetic cards and OTP chip cards. OTP magnetic cards utilize traditional magnetic strip technology to display dynamic passwords for secure transactions. In contrast, OTP chip cards integrate advanced microchip technology, which enhances security by generating unique passwords directly from the card. Both types serve to bolster authentication processes across various platforms, offering users enhanced protection against fraud and unauthorized access while facilitating seamless online and offline transactions.</p></p>
