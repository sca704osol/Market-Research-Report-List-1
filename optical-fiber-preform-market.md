<p><p>The optical fiber preform market is segmented into various manufacturing techniques, including Vapor Axial Deposition (VAD), Outside Vapor Deposition (OVD), Plasma Activated Chemical Vapor Deposition (PCVD), and Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition (MCVD). VAD uses a controlled vapor to build the preform layer by layer, while OVD employs an external deposition technique. PCVD utilizes plasma for chemical reactions, enhancing material properties, and MCVD involves modifying the internal structure of a silica tube for precision in fiber production. These processes cater to diverse application requirements in telecommunications and data transmission.</p></p>
