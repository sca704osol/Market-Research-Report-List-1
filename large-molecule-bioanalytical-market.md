<p><p>The Large Molecule Bioanalytical market encompasses three key areas: ADME (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, and Excretion), PK (Pharmacokinetics), and PD (Pharmacodynamics). ADME evaluates how drugs interact with biological systems; PK studies how the body affects a drug over time, focusing on parameters like concentration and half-life; while PD investigates the drug's effects on the body, including mechanisms of action. Together, they provide a comprehensive understanding of a drug's efficacy, safety, and overall behavior in clinical development.</p></p>
