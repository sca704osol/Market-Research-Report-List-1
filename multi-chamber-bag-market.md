<p><p>The multi-chamber bag market is categorized into three main types based on the number of chambers. Two-chamber bags are designed for simple dual-compartment applications, allowing separate storage of incompatible products. Three-chamber bags offer enhanced versatility, enabling more complex formulations or mixing of components just before use. Bags with more than three chambers provide advanced capabilities for storing multiple formulations, catering to sophisticated pharmaceutical or cosmetic applications, ensuring stability and precision in dosing, and enhancing product efficacy.</p></p>
