<p><p>Chitosan edible packaging films are categorized into three crystal forms: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form offers high solubility and flexibility, making it suitable for moisture-sensitive products. The β crystal form provides enhanced mechanical strength and barrier properties, ideal for extending shelf life. Meanwhile, the γ crystal form showcases unique processing capabilities, allowing for customization in thickness and texture. Each type serves specific needs in food preservation, promoting sustainability and reducing waste in packaging solutions.</p></p>
