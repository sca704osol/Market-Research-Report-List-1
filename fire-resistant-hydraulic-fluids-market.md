<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Hydraulic Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant hydraulic fluids market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are aqueous solutions with low viscosity, suitable for non-combustible applications. HFB fluids are water-containing but with higher viscosity, offering moderate fire resistance. HFC fluids are water-containing synthetic fluids that provide good fire resistance and are used in demanding environments. HFD fluids are high-temperature resistant, offering superior fire safety in hydrocarbon-based systems, making them essential in industries like manufacturing, mining, and aerospace.</p></p>
