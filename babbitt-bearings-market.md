<p><p>Babbitt bearings are primarily used in machinery for ensuring smooth operation and reducing friction. The market includes self-equalizing bearings, which adjust to align with shaft misalignments; sleeve bearings, characterized by a cylindrical design that supports radial loads; thrust bearings, designed to handle axial loads; tapered bearings, which manage both radial and axial loads through a unique shape; and tilting shoe bearings, featuring pivoting elements that optimize load distribution. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing machine performance and reliability.</p></p>
