<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market primarily includes two types of image sensor technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high-quality imaging and low noise but typically consume more power. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer faster processing, lower power consumption, and integration capabilities, making them popular in mobile devices. The demand for advanced imaging solutions drives innovation in both segments, catering to various consumer electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
