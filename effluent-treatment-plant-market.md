<p><ul><li>Preliminary Treatment</li><li>Secondary or Biological Treatment</li><li>Tertiary or Advanced or Disinfection Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The effluent treatment plant market comprises three key types. Preliminary treatment involves the initial screening and removal of large solids and debris from wastewater. Secondary or biological treatment focuses on breaking down organic matter using microbial processes, effectively reducing pollutants. Tertiary or advanced treatment includes additional processes like filtration and disinfection to ensure the treated effluent meets quality standards for reuse or safe discharge. Each stage plays a vital role in protecting the environment and public health.</p></p>
