<p><p>The single-cell genome sequencing technology market encompasses various methodologies, including Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), quantitative PCR (qPCR), microarrays, and Multiple Displacement Amplification (MDA). NGS provides high-throughput sequencing, enabling comprehensive genomic analysis. PCR and qPCR allow for targeted amplification and quantitative measurement of specific DNA sequences. Microarrays facilitate the simultaneous analysis of multiple genes, while MDA enables efficient genome amplification from single cells, enhancing downstream analyses. Together, these technologies cater to diverse research needs in genomics.</p></p>
