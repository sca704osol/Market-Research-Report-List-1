<p><ul><li>Fixed Implants</li><li>Variable Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humeral implant market is categorized into fixed and variable implants. Fixed implants are designed for specific anatomical configurations and provide stable, long-term fixation, making them suitable for patients requiring consistent shoulder function. In contrast, variable implants offer adjustable features that allow surgeons to customize the fit and alignment during the procedure. This flexibility increases adaptability to varying patient anatomies and surgical needs, making variable implants a preferred choice in complex cases where precision is critical for optimal outcomes.</p></p>
