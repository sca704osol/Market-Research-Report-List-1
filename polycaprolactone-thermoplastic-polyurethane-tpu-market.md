<p><p>The Polycaprolactone Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) market comprises several key components, including polyols, diisocyanates, and diols. Polyols are long-chain polymers that provide flexibility and durability in TPU formulations. Diisocyanates are reactive agents that bond with polyols to form the polymer structure, influencing mechanical and thermal properties. Diols serve as chain extenders, enhancing the TPU's strength and elasticity. Together, these materials contribute to the production of high-performance TPUs used in various applications like automotive, footwear, and medical devices.</p></p>
