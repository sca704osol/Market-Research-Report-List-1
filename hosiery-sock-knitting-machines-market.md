<p><p>The hosiery and sock knitting machines market comprises two main types: circular sock knitting machines and flat sock knitting machines. Circular sock knitting machines utilize a continuous, round needle configuration, allowing for efficient production of seamless socks and hosiery with varying patterns and designs. In contrast, flat sock knitting machines generate flat knitted fabrics, which are then sewn into socks, offering greater versatility in style and customization. Both types cater to diverse market demands, ensuring high-quality hosiery production.</p></p>
