<p><strong>The Upright Commercial Vacuums Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-motor</li><li>Dual-motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The upright commercial vacuums market is categorized into single-motor and dual-motor types. Single-motor vacuums use one motor to drive both suction and brush functions, making them lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for small spaces. In contrast, dual-motor vacuums utilize separate motors for suction and brush operation, enhancing cleaning performance on various surfaces. This dual approach provides stronger suction and improved brush action, making them suitable for larger areas or more demanding cleaning tasks in commercial environments.</p></p>
