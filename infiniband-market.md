<p><strong>The InfiniBand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Data Rate</li><li>Double Data Rate</li><li>Quad Data Rate</li><li>Fourteen Data Rate</li><li>Enhanced Data Rate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>InfiniBand market types are categorized based on their data transmission rates. Single Data Rate (SDR) offers a bandwidth of 2.5 Gbps, while Double Data Rate (DDR) doubles this to 5 Gbps. Quad Data Rate (QDR) further amplifies it to 10 Gbps, and Fourteen Data Rate (FDR) reaches 14 Gbps. Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) boasts a higher throughput of 25 Gbps. These variations cater to diverse application requirements, ranging from high-performance computing to data center interconnects.</p></p>
