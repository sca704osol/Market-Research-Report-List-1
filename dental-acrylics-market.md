<p><p>The dental acrylics market comprises various types utilized in prosthetics and restorative dentistry. Heat cure acrylic resin is used for durable and long-lasting dental appliances, requiring heat for polymerization. Self-curing acrylic resin activates through a chemical reaction, allowing for easy application. Cold cure acrylic resin also sets at room temperature but offers a faster process. Light-cured acrylic resin utilizes light for quick polymerization, enhancing precision. Other types may include specialized formulations for unique applications, catering to diverse dental needs.</p></p>
