<p><ul><li>Sinker EDM</li><li>Wire EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) machines market is primarily divided into two types: Sinker EDM and Wire EDM. Sinker EDM, also known as plunge EDM, utilizes a shaped electrode to create complex cavities and features in metal workpieces by eroding material through electrical discharge. In contrast, Wire EDM employs a fine wire electrode to cut intricate shapes and contours with high precision, making it ideal for producing delicate components and maintaining tight tolerances in various industries.</p></p>
