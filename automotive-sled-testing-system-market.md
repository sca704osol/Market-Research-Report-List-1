<p><p>The Automotive Sled Testing System market comprises two primary types: Decelerator Sled Testing Systems and Catapult Sled Testing Systems. Decelerator sleds evaluate vehicle safety by simulating crash conditions, allowing for the assessment of restraint systems and impact responses. In contrast, catapult sleds launch vehicles or crash test dummies at high speeds to test dynamic safety features and structural integrity. Both systems are crucial for automotive manufacturers to ensure compliance with safety regulations and enhance vehicle performance in crash scenarios.</p></p>
