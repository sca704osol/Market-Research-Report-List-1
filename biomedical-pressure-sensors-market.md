<p><p>The biomedical pressure sensors market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Capacitive sensors detect pressure through capacitance change, while fiber optic sensors utilize light transmission for accurate measurements. Telemetric sensors enable wireless data transmission, enhancing patient mobility. Piezoresistive sensors employ resistance change in response to pressure, offering precise readings. Additionally, "Others" includes emerging technologies like microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) and hybrid sensors. Each type plays a crucial role in medical diagnostics, wearable devices, and healthcare monitoring solutions.</p></p>
