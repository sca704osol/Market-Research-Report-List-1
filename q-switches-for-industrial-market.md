<p><p>Q-switches are crucial components in laser technology, used to produce high-intensity laser pulses. In the industrial market, active Q-switches utilize an external control mechanism to vary the laser's gain, allowing for precise pulse generation and high energy output. Conversely, passive Q-switches rely on materials that absorb energy and then release it in short bursts, offering simplicity and robustness in design. Both types facilitate various applications, including material processing, marking, and engraving, enhancing efficiency in industrial operations.</p></p>
