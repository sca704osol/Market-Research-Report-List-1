<p><strong>The Veterinary Ultrasound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2D Ultrasound</li><li>3D & 4D Ultrasound</li><li>Doppler Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary ultrasound market includes various types that cater to different diagnostic needs. 2D ultrasound provides two-dimensional images, ideal for basic assessments like checking organ structures and detecting abnormalities. 3D and 4D ultrasounds offer three-dimensional imaging, with 4D adding real-time motion, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and understanding of anatomical details. Doppler ultrasound focuses on blood flow analysis, assisting in the detection of cardiovascular issues in animals. These technologies collectively enhance veterinary diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
