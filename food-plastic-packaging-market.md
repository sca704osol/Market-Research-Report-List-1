<p><p>The food plastic packaging market consists of three main types: thermoset polymers, thermoplastic polymers, and elastomers. Thermoset polymers offer durability and heat resistance, making them suitable for rigid containers. Thermoplastic polymers, which are versatile and can be reshaped upon heating, are widely used for flexible packaging. Elastomers provide excellent elasticity and sealability, ideal for stretchable wrappers. Together, these materials enhance food preservation, extend shelf life, and meet diverse packaging needs in the food industry.</p></p>
