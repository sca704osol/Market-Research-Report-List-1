<p><p>The Si Attenuators market is segmented into Super High Frequency (SHF) and Ultra High Frequency (UHF) types. UHF attenuators, spanning 300 MHz to 3 GHz, are prevalent in television broadcasting, two-way radios, and wireless communications.
