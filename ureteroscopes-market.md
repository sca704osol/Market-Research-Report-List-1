<p><p>The ureteroscope market is categorized into three main types: flexible, semi-rigid, and rigid ureteroscopes. Flexible ureteroscopes are designed for enhanced maneuverability and are ideal for accessing challenging areas within the urinary tract. Semi-rigid ureteroscopes combine attributes of both flexible and rigid types, offering moderate flexibility while maintaining some structural rigidity. Rigid ureteroscopes provide a stable platform for visualization and interventions in the urinary system, making them suitable for straightforward procedures. Each type serves specific clinical needs and scenarios in urology.</p></p>
