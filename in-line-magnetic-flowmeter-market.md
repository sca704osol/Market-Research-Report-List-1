<p><ul><li>DC Magnetic Flowmeter</li><li>Induction Magnetic Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line magnetic flowmeters are essential instruments for measuring the flow of conductive liquids. The DC magnetic flowmeter operates using direct current to generate a magnetic field, ideal for low flow rates and varying fluid properties. In contrast, the induction magnetic flowmeter utilizes alternating current to create a dynamic magnetic field, providing accurate measurements across a wider range of flow rates and applications. Both types are crucial in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing, ensuring reliable flow monitoring.</p></p>
