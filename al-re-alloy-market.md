<p><p>The Al-RE alloy market comprises two primary types: single eutectic and hypereutectic alloys. Single eutectic alloys, formed at a specific composition, feature a uniform microstructure that enhances strength and ductility, making them suitable for aerospace applications. In contrast, hypereutectic alloys contain a higher proportion of reinforcing phases, providing superior wear resistance and hardness, ideal for automotive and industrial uses. Both types cater to distinct industry needs, leveraging their unique properties for enhanced performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
