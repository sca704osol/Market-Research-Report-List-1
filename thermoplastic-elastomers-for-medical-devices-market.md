Thermoplastic polyolefins provide superior chemical resistance, while thermoplastic polyurethanes feature enhanced abrasion resistance and elasticity. Polyether ester TPEs (TPEE) combine the advantages of both polyether and polyester materials, delivering good hydrolytic stability. Other TPE variants cater to specific application needs, contributing to a versatile medical device market.</p></p>
