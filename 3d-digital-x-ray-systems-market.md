<p><strong>The 3D Digital X-Ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Digital X-Ray Systems market is primarily divided into two types: CR (Computed Radiography) and DR (Digital Radiography) technologies. CR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems use photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, which are then processed digitally, offering a balance of cost-effectiveness and image quality. DR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems, on the other hand, utilize flat-panel detectors for direct image capture, providing faster image acquisition, improved resolution, and enhanced workflow efficiency, making them increasingly popular in clinical settings.</p></p>
