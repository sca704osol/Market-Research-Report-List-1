<p><p>The nephrology fiber lasers market comprises three primary types: Holmium YAG lasers, Thulium YAG lasers, and Erbium YAG lasers. Holmium YAG lasers are widely used for procedures like stone fragmentation due to their efficiency in delivering energy. Thulium YAG lasers offer versatile applications for soft tissue procedures, providing precise cutting and coagulation. Erbium YAG lasers, known for their ability to remove tissue with minimal thermal damage, are increasingly adopted for delicate surgeries, enhancing patient safety and recovery.</p></p>
