<p><strong>The Balls Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ball Screw</li><li>External Circulation Ball Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The balls screw market is primarily divided into two types: internal circulation ball screws and external circulation ball screws. Internal circulation ball screws have ball bearings that circulate within the screw assembly, providing smooth operation and efficient space usage, ideal for compact applications. In contrast, external circulation ball screws feature a return mechanism outside the screw, allowing for longer screw lengths and facilitating higher load capacities, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications in various industries.</p></p>
