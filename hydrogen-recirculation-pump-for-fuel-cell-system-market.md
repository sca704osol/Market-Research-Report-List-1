<p><ul><li>Roots</li><li>Claw</li><li>Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen recirculation pump market for fuel cell systems consists of three main types: Roots pumps, Claw pumps, and Scroll pumps. Roots pumps utilize a positive displacement mechanism, efficiently moving large volumes of hydrogen with minimal pulsation. Claw pumps, also positive displacement, rely on interlocking rotors to compress gas while offering a compact design and low vibrations. Scroll pumps compress hydrogen using spiral-shaped scrolls, providing smooth operation and high efficiency, making them suitable for various fuel cell applications. Each type has distinct advantages tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
