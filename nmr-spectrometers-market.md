The 600 MHz models offer even higher sensitivity, crucial for biomolecular studies. Ranging from 700-750 MHz and 800-850 MHz is optimal for advanced research, and 900+ MHz instruments provide state-of-the-art capabilities for high-resolution analysis in demanding applications.</p></p>
